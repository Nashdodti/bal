After spending a lifetime loving each other, Melody and Louis are separated when death comes for the latter. For Mélodie, now in mourning, life goes on waiting. ]]> A first live session music video for 2Frères

To pay tribute to the sweet melody composed by Sonny Caouette for the song M’aimerais-tu pareil? by 2Frères, nine musicians gathered in a studio to bring the song to life. A live recording, which is not on the album, and which later touched thousands of Quebecers. A hymn to love that lingers through time, softly and simply.

A total of four violins, a double bass, drums, a guitar, a grand piano and a singer are in the Circuit-Est dance studio to deliver a performance of the track released a few months earlier.

It is under the supervision of Julien Bouchard and Steve Lemay from Studio London that the live recording takes place.

The footage had to be filmed very smoothly in order to blend in with the sweet melody performed by the whole band. With more than 5 million views, the music video for M’aimerais-tu pareil? continues to reach audiences across Quebec.

]]> Eating local at work or school: easier than you think!

Jo Côté visits the province to meet the “champions” of the Aliments du Québec au menu program. One of the institutions that stands out is the daycare centre La Petite-Patrie by serving more than 51% Quebec foods on their menu. Jo, curious by nature, wonders about the implementation of such a program in an institution like a daycare center and the benefits that such a measure has on children and the local economy.

This video was produced as part of a 6 episode mini-series. The series aims to show how easy it is to source and consume local food in a large institution. The series presents the program already established in several institutions including a university, a hospital, a company, a high school, a CEGEP and of course, a daycare centre.

In January 2020, the campaign won the DUX Grand Prize in the category “Communication Campaign – SME and Large NPOs”. The DUX awards recognize the leadership of agri-food companies, NPOs, institutions and non-food commercial enterprises that have innovated to help improve the population’s diet.
